Victoria Brook

Executive Director

Victoria Brook is the Executive Director of Arkose. She aims to advance high-quality research on large-scale AI risks by facilitating people's entry into the field. Victoria has authored research guides and led courses for researchers and students focusing on AI safety. She also brings experience from various operational roles in small organizations.
