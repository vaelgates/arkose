This seems a possibility. There are several trends suggesting that advanced systems may exist within the next 50 years:

  • We have been working on AI since 1950. The deep learning revolution started in 2012. On the scale of human history (e.g. Agricultural Revolution ~12,000 years ago, Industrial Revolution ~200 years ago), this is not much time, and investments increased massively since 2010.
  • We now have unprecedented and unpredicted progress in large language models like GPT-3 and PaLM. For example, PaLM is able to explain novel jokes in two-shot prompts.
  • PaLM has also shown that new capabilities can emerge entirely as a function of scale, without major innovations in the underlying paradigm. Prior research suggested this was a possibility, but PaLM has demonstrated that this still holds at very large model size and with high amounts of training data.
