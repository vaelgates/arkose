Indeed, it is somewhat plausible that we would get these advanced systems well before the end of the 21st century. Reasons for that include:
  • We have been working on AI since 1950. The deep learning revolution started in 2012. This is not much time, and investments investments increased massively since 2010.
  • We now have unprecedented, and unpredicted progress in large language models like GPT-3 and PaLM. For example, PaLM is able to explain novel jokes in two-shot propts.
  • PaLM has also shown that new capabilities can emerge just as a function of scale, without major innovations in the underlying paradigm required.
